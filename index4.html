Certified Course In IT (CIT)

Complete beginner-to-intermediate IT certification covering computer fundamentals, internet & digital literacy, basic MS Office tools, and essential cyber security awareness — perfect foundation for jobs, further studies, or government exams.

What you'll learn:
  • Computer Fundamentals: Hardware, software, OS, file management
  • Internet & Web Basics: Browsing, email, online safety, digital literacy
  • MS Office Introduction: Word, Excel, PowerPoint essentials
  • Cyber Security & Awareness: Threats, passwords, data protection, safe practices
Beginner to Intermediate Duration: 6 Months

Diploma Course In IT (DIT)

Comprehensive 6-month Diploma in Information Technology covering complete computer fundamentals, Operating System, advanced MS Office tools, Internet skills, and essential digital literacy — perfect for job preparation and government exams.

Course Content:
  • 1. Fundamentals of Computer
  • 2. Operating System (Windows)
  • 3. M.S. Office (2010 Version)
  • 4. Internet
  • 5. CD & Pen Drive use
  • 6. Print Out and Scanning
  • 7. Concept of Virus & Antivirus
  • 8. Typing Master
Beginner to Intermediate Duration: 6 Months

Advance Diploma In IT (ADIT)

Advanced level Diploma in Information Technology with two semesters covering web designing, graphic design, accounting software, and programming languages.
